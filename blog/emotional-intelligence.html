What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence refers to the ability to see and manage the emotions of the self and others. This breaks down into a number of different skills, which makes emotional intelligence a very broad concept, capturing almost every positive human trait apart from raw processing power.

Why is it important?

Intuitively, it seems that the relationship between EQ, IQ and performance is more likely multiplicative than additive.

EG:

Outcome = EQ x IQ

Vs

Outcome = EQ + IQ

This implies that someone with very high intelligence but very low EQ will do much worse than someone with with average intelligence and good EQ.
